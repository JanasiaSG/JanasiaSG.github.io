BATMAN

I AM VENGEANCE. I AM THE NIGHT. I AM BATMAN.

Bruce Wayne is the Batman. As a child, he witnessed the murders of his parents, Thomas and Martha Wayne in Crime Alley in Gotham. He then vowed to wage war on crime and he did so by training. He tested his limits and willpower and pushed his body to near perfection. He is considered to be one of the greatist fighters and martial artists in the DC Universe and is known as the World's Greatist Detective. In his war against crime, he has taken up proteges, known as robins. The most notable protege is Dick Grayson, the first Robin (Now is Nightwing). Though Batman may be human, he has defeated many heroes and villains who are much stronger and powerful than he is. He is also a founding member of the Justice League. But in Gotham, he has a team that he regards as his family known as the Bat-Family. It mainly consists of Dick Grasyon, Jason Todd(Currently Red Hood and 2nd Robin), Tim Drake (3rd Robin;Formerly Red Robin;Currently, Robin), Barbara Gordon (Batgirl & Oracle), Stephanie Brown (Spoiler), Cassandra Cain (Orphan), Damian Wayne (Robin), Duke Thomas (The Signal), Kate Kane (Batwoman) and who can forget about Alfred!.
